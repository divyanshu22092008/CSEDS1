What is polluion?
Pollution-
Pollution is the harmful contamination of the environment by harmful substances, posing a grave threat to ecosystems and human health. Key types include air, water, and soil pollution, primarily caused by industrial processes, vehicle emissions, and waste. Effects range from respiratory diseases to loss of biodiversity. Addressing this crisis requires collective action, including adopting sustainable practices like reducing, reusing, and recycling waste, planting trees, and switching to cleaner energy.
